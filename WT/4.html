Beverages and Their Descriptions

Coffee
A brewed beverage made from roasted coffee beans, known for its rich flavor and caffeine content.
Tea
A hot or cold beverage made by steeping cured or fresh tea leaves in water, often enjoyed for its variety of flavors.
Juice
A drink made from the extraction or pressing of the natural liquid contained in fruits and vegetables.
Soda
A carbonated soft drink that is sweetened and flavored, often enjoyed as a refreshing beverage.
Water
The essential liquid for life, often consumed plain or flavored with fruits and herbs.
